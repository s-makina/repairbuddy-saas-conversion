Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about 99SmartX pricing

Can I try 99SmartX for free?

Absolutely! Every plan comes with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required. You'll have full access to all features in your chosen plan during the trial period.

Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged the prorated difference immediately. Downgrading takes effect at the next billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and PayPal. Enterprise customers can also pay via bank transfer or invoice.

Is there a contract or can I cancel anytime?

There are no long-term contracts. You can cancel your subscription at any time. Your account will remain active until the end of your current billing period.

Do you offer discounts for annual billing?

Yes! When you choose annual billing, you save 20% compared to monthly billing. That's like getting over 2 months free each year.
